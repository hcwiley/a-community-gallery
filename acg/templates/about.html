What's going on here?


This is a long term group performance piece. I am instigating rapid consuming and sharing of art in a public space. Anyone can come to the website and upload their artwork. All of the works will then be displayed in virtual gallery on the St Joe Loft's gallery TV. Using the software and interface I created anyone will be able to walk up to the TV and start moving through the art and examining individual works with simple gestures.
